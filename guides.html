How to Download New App (Text)

  1. Go to the Downloader app.
  2. Type into the Downloader app 747509.
  3. Click Install (bottom right corner).
  4. Once done, click Open (bottom right).
  5. Click Allow.
  6. Click OK on the remote.
  7. Click Edit.
  8. Change the playlist name to whatever you want.
  9. Add your login details.
  10. Click Update Playlist.
  11. Click OK on the pop-up window.
  12. Click OK on the remote again.
  13. Click Connect.
